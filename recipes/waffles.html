Waffles

A waffle is a popular dish made from leavened batter or dough cooked between two patterned plates in a waffle iron, creating a distinctive grid-like, crispy exterior and a soft interior.

Originating in Europe and perfected in Belgium, they are commonly served for breakfast or dessert, often topped with syrup, fruit, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract

Steps to prepare

  1. Gather Ingredients and Tools
  2. Mix the Batter
  3. Preheat and Grease the Waffle Iron
  4. Cook the Waffles
  5. Serve and Keep Warm
  6. Enjoy!
