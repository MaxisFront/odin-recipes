Breaded Milanesa

Milanesa is a beloved Latin American and Italian-influenced dish consisting of thin, tenderized slices of beef, chicken, or pork, dredged in flour, dipped in seasoned egg wash, and coated in breadcrumbs or cracker meal before being shallow-fried to a golden crisp.

It is incredibly delicious due to its perfect contrast of a crunchy, savory exterior with a tender, juicy interior, enhanced by seasonings like garlic, onion, and parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Tenderized meat
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying

Steps to prepare

  1. Prepare the meat
  2. Set up the Breading Station
  3. Bread the Cutlets
  4. Fry the Milanesa
  5. Serve
  6. Enjoy!
