Boiled Eggs

Boiled eggs are whole chicken eggs cooked in their shells by immersion in boiling water, resulting in solid whites and yolks ranging from runny to firm depending on cooking time

They are a versatile, high-protein food used globally, commonly categorized as soft-boiled (4-6 min) with liquid yolks, or hard-boiled (10-12 min) with fully set, firm yolks.

Ingredients

  • Fridge-cold eggs
  • Water
  • Ice

Steps to prepare

  1. Boil water
  2. Add eggs
  3. Reduce heat
  4. Set Timer (Approx. 10 min.)
  5. Ice Bath
  6. Peel
  7. Enjoy!
